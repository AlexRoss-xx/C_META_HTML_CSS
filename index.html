Welcome to Lucky Shrub

Nestled in the heart of the picturesque city of Tucson, Arizona, Lucky Shrub emerges as a distinguished medium-sized garden design firm, weaving a tapestry of horticultural artistry that transcends the ordinary. With an unwavering commitment to transforming landscapes into captivating sanctuaries, Lucky Shrub stands as a beacon of excellence in the realms of garden design, creation, meticulous maintenance, and sophisticated landscaping.

Beyond the mere cultivation of greenery, Lucky Shrub has cultivated an immersive experience for its clientele by seamlessly blending art and nature. The firm's visionary approach extends to the establishment of a quaint yet thriving plant nursery, where a curated selection of both indoor and outdoor flora awaits. This nursery serves as a verdant haven for enthusiasts and novices alike, beckoning them to explore the diverse botanical wonders that the world of Lucky Shrub has to offer.

As a comprehensive one-stop shop, Lucky Shrub empowers its clients to embark on a journey of transformative possibilities, turning any space into an oasis that evokes both admiration and pride. The firm's dedication to impeccable craftsmanship and a holistic understanding of botanical ecosystems positions them as purveyors of not merely gardens, but living, breathing works of art.

From the initial conceptualization of a garden's design to the meticulous execution of landscaping projects, Lucky Shrub approaches each endeavor with a harmonious blend of creativity and technical expertise. The result is an embodiment of nature's finest elements coalescing into a symphony of colors, textures, and fragrances that captivate the senses and elevate the aesthetics of any environment.

In the dynamic landscape of Lucky Shrub, every project is a unique canvas awaiting the strokes of the horticultural artisans. Through a collaborative process that involves understanding the client's vision, Lucky Shrub crafts bespoke green masterpieces that reflect individuality and resonate with the soul of the space.

In essence, Lucky Shrub transcends the conventional boundaries of garden-related services, offering an immersive and transformative journey into the realm where nature and design converge. This Tucson-based haven redefines the art of botanical expression, leaving an indelible mark on the landscapes it touches and ensuring that every client can bask in the pride of an oasis that is truly their own.

Our Services

Garden Design and Creation

Lucky Shrub's team of skilled designers takes pride in conceptualizing and bringing to life stunning garden designs. Tailored to each client's vision, these designs encompass a harmonious blend of aesthetic appeal, functionality, and sustainability.

Maintenance Expertise

The commitment to excellence doesn't end with the initial design. Lucky Shrub provides meticulous garden maintenance services to ensure that the created spaces flourish and evolve over time. From regular pruning and weeding to seasonal plant care, the company's dedicated maintenance team ensures that every garden continues to thrive.

Comprehensive Landscaping Services

Beyond individual gardens, Lucky Shrub offers full-scale landscaping services. Whether it's revamping existing landscapes or starting from scratch, the company leverages its expertise to craft outdoor environments that captivate the senses. This includes hardscaping elements, water features, and innovative planting schemes to enhance the overall appeal.

Lucky Shrub

Lucky Shrub blossomed from the passion and expertise of its founders, Jason and Maria, a dynamic husband-and-wife duo united by their deep-rooted love for plants and landscaping. With a shared vision and a commitment to creating vibrant and thriving outdoor spaces, they embarked on a journey to establish a company that would blend Jason's innovative design skills with Maria's strategic marketing acumen.

As the garden architect of Lucky Shrub, Jason brings years of experience and a keen eye for detail to the forefront. His role extends beyond conceptualizing stunning designs; he actively leads a dedicated team of landscapers, ensuring that every project is executed with precision and a touch of botanical brilliance. From conceptualization to completion, Jason's creative vision serves as the driving force behind Lucky Shrub's transformative landscapes.

Complementing Jason's green thumb, Maria takes charge of the company's marketing endeavors and oversees the bustling nursery. Her strategic approach to branding and promotion plays a pivotal role in Lucky Shrub's growth and visibility. With a finger on the pulse of market trends and a commitment to excellence, Maria ensures that Lucky Shrub not only meets but exceeds the expectations of its clientele.

Together, Jason and Maria form the nucleus of Lucky Shrub, infusing their shared passion for plants into every aspect of the business. Whether it's designing a picturesque garden retreat, curating an extensive collection of plants at the nursery, or implementing effective marketing strategies, Lucky Shrub stands as a testament to their dedication to creating beautiful and sustainable outdoor environments. The harmonious synergy between Jason's design prowess and Maria's marketing finesse has propelled Lucky Shrub into a flourishing venture, where the love for plants is not just a business, but a way of life.
